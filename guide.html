🔑 Logging In
1
Open the app

Access the link on your phone browser. Bookmark it for quick access.

2
Sign in with Google

Tap Sign in with Google and use your authorised account. Only approved accounts can access the app.

💡 Stay signed in — the app remembers your session. You only need to sign in again if you manually sign out.

After logging in you will see the attendance screen defaulting to today's date and the current TOD (time of day).

✅ Marking Attendance

Tap the Platoon tab (Platoon 1 / Platoon 2 / HQ) to switch between platoons. Personnel are grouped by section.

Setting a Status

1
Quick buttons

Each row has quick-tap buttons for the most common statuses (Present, S/O, More). Tap once to assign.

2
More statuses

Tap ▾ More on a person's row to see all available statuses from a dropdown.

3
Date-range statuses (MC, Leave, AMB, etc.)

After selecting a date-range status, enter the From and Till dates. The status auto-carries across TODs and future days within the range.

Calling a Person

Each row shows a 📞 Call button if the person has a phone number on file. Tap it to dial directly. The number is not displayed on screen for data protection.

Clearing Statuses

Tap ✕ Clear [Platoon] Statuses at the bottom of a platoon section, then confirm. This resets everyone in that platoon to unassigned.

⚠ Clearing is irreversible. Only clear if you need to start over for that platoon.

Set All Present

Tap the ✓ All Present button to mark everyone in the platoon as Present in one tap. Then individually update those who are not.

🔍 Search

Tap the 🔍 button in the top header to open the search modal. You can search by:

  • Name — e.g. "Jakin", "Ronald"
  • Rank — e.g. "3SG", "CPL"
  • Status — e.g. type "MC" to see everyone on MC, "OL" for overseas leave, "duty" for all duty personnel
  • Detail field — e.g. a date or a detail note

Quick Edit from Search

1
Tap a result

The modal closes, the app jumps to that person's platoon tab and scrolls to their row.

2
Row unlocks automatically

Even if the platoon is already submitted, the row is temporarily unlocked (highlighted in amber) for quick editing.

3
Tap ✓ Done

The row re-locks once you're finished. The platoon remains submitted.

💡 Use this when you need to correct one person after a platoon has already been submitted — no need to unlock the whole platoon.

🔄 Auto Carry-Forward

The app automatically fills in statuses when you switch TODs or dates, so you don't have to re-enter everything.

Whole-Day Duty — carries across all TODs on the same day

Set once at HLS/FP and it automatically appears in MP, LP, and Night Strength:

DUTY DTO ADTL DOO DTL

Date-Range Statuses — carries across days

Statuses with a from/till date carry across all TODs for all days within the date range:

MC HL OL LL ARF AMB T1 AMB T2 and all other leave statuses

Night Strength — S/O auto-mark

Personnel with S/O or S/O Till (within date range) are automatically marked at the 2030/2230 TOD.

💡 Carry only fills in people who have no status yet for that slot. If you manually set a different status, it will not be overwritten.
💡 Always enter the From date (first day sick/leave) and Till date (last day) so carry-forward works correctly across days.
🌙 Night Strength

Night Strength (2030/2230) has special behaviour compared to other TODs:

Statuses Ending Today Are Cleared

If someone's MC, OFF, Leave, or any date-range status has a till date of today, their status is automatically left blank at Night Strength. This means they are back in camp and S/I.

💡 Exception: S/O Till ending today is not cleared — the person is still out tonight and reports back the next morning at 0800.

S/O Auto-Mark

All permanent S/O and active S/O Till personnel are automatically filled in when you open Night Strength.

Night Strength Telegram — Stay Out Section

The Node Message at Night Strength includes a Stay Out section listing all S/O personnel:

Stay Out: 4 1. CPL VINITH (PERM) 2. 3WO RONALD (PERM) 3. PTE NIHAL TILL 160426 4. PTE PRAVEER TILL 230426
⚠️ Expiry Alerts

A yellow banner appears at the top of the attendance screen when someone's leave or status is ending soon.

IconMeaning
🔴Status ends today
🟡Status ends tomorrow

For S/O Till, the banner also shows the reporting time:

🔴 LCP JAKIN — Stay Out Till ends tdy, back tmr 0800 (S/O) 🟡 PTE GORDON — Stay Out Till ends tmr, back in 2 days 0800 (S/O)
💡 Use this banner to follow up — confirm the person is extending their MC/leave, or update their status for the next day.
✍️ Submitting Attendance

Once all statuses for a platoon are filled in, scroll to the bottom of that platoon tab and tap:

Save & Submit [Platoon] Attendance

  • The platoon is locked — no further changes can be made
  • A green ✏ Edit button appears for admin/authorised users to unlock and correct
  • Submit each platoon separately (P1, P2, HQ)
⚠ Submit only when all statuses are confirmed. Submitting locks the platoon.
💡 Need to correct one person after submitting? Use the 🔍 Search to jump directly to their row — it temporarily unlocks just that person without unlocking the whole platoon.
📤 Telegram Messages

Tap the Telegram tab (bottom nav) to generate messages. There are four message types arranged in a 2×2 grid:

📤 Node Message

Full parade state for the whole node. Sent to the node group chat. Includes:

  • Total strength and duty count with names and roles e.g. 1. 3SG SYAZHAZIQ (DTL)
  • All statuses with names listed
  • At Night Strength: a Stay Out section with (PERM) or TILL date
  • You can adjust Total Node Strength manually and add Additional Notes

👑 Comd Message

Commanders-only view (SCT and above). Shows only commanders' statuses. Sent to the commanders' group chat.

🏥 MC List

Lists all personnel currently on MC or Hospitalisation Leave, with platoon and section:

Node: JRN Date: 130426 MC 1. LCP JAKIN, PLT 2 TO BASE MC 130426 - 130426 2. PTE BRAXTON, PLT 1 TO BASE HL 110426 - 310526

📊 Quick Summary

A compact count of all non-zero statuses — useful for a quick check before sending the full message:

JRN PARADE STATE 13 Apr 26 (NIGHT STR) Total strength: 127 Duty Personnel : 5 Present : 85 Stay Out : 17 MC : 4 HL : 2 OFF : 4 ────────────────────── Total accounted : 117 / 127

The total turns green when it tallies with node strength, red if not.

Tap 📋 Copy Message for Telegram to copy, then paste into the Telegram group.

💡 The message is generated live from current attendance records. Make sure all statuses are filled in before copying.
🌙 S/O Consolidated List

Tap the 🌙 button in the top header to open the Stay Out list.

This shows all personnel currently on S/O or S/O Till, grouped by platoon, with:

  • Permanent S/O badge
  • Temporary S/O Till — end date and days remaining countdown
💡 Use this as a quick reference before Night Strength to know who will be auto-marked S/O.
📋 Nominal Roll (NR) Mode

NR mode is for events like CST, IPPT, SOC, or any activity that needs a separate attendance record outside the normal parade state.

Starting an NR

1
Select Nominal Roll on the home screen

Choose NR from the TOD options.

2
Enter the event name

E.g. "IPPT", "CST", "SOC". This appears in the NR telegram message header.

3
Statuses are pre-filled automatically

The NR loads statuses from the most recently completed parade state (full strength, e.g. 127/127). If MP is fully done and LP is not, it uses MP. This saves you from re-entering everything.

4
Mark attendance and submit

Update anyone whose status differs for this event, then submit each platoon.

⚠ Changes made in an NR do not affect the parade states. Setting someone as Present in the NR will not change their MC status in the parade state.

NR Telegram Message

The NR Telegram tab generates a formatted CAA message:

IPPT CAA 13 Apr 26 Total No. Of Strength: 127 Total No. Of Commanders including TLs : 42 Total No. Of NSFs excluding TLs : 85 —————— Reason For Not Attending: MC (Medical Certificate): 4 1. LCP IZZAD 2. PTE GORDON —————— Commanders Completed: 41/42 (98%) NSFs Completed: 83/85 (98%) JRN Completion rate: 98%
💡 SCT and above (including 3SG, 2SG, etc.) are counted as Commanders. REC to CFC are counted as NSFs.
🕓 History

Tap History in the bottom nav to view past parade states.

  • Tap a date to Preview the parade state PDF
  • Tap Edit to go back to that date's attendance (admin only for past dates)
  • Download a date range as a bulk PDF (admin feature)
  • Admin can Delete a history entry — deleted entries stay deleted and will not reappear after re-login
💡 History is read-only for normal users. Contact your admin if you need to correct a past record.
💡 Quick Tips
  • Always mark attendance at HLS/FP first — duty statuses carry forward to all other TODs automatically
  • For MC/Leave, always enter both From and Till dates so carry-forward works correctly
  • Check the expiry banner at the start of each parade state — it flags anyone whose status ends that day or tomorrow
  • At Night Strength, statuses ending today are automatically cleared — the person shows as blank (back in camp tomorrow)
  • Use 🔍 Search to find anyone by name or status — type "MC" to see all on MC instantly
  • Tap a search result to jump directly to that person's row and quick-edit even after submission
  • The 📊 Quick Summary telegram tab is a fast way to verify your total tally before sending the full message
  • Do not submit until all statuses are confirmed — submitted platoons are locked
