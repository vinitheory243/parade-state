🔑 Logging In
1
Open the app

Access the link on your phone browser. Bookmark it for quick access.

2
Sign in with Google

Tap Sign in with Google and use your authorised account. Only approved accounts can access the app.

💡 Stay signed in — the app remembers your session. You only need to sign in again if you manually sign out.
🏠 Home Screen

After logging in you will see the attendance screen defaulting to today's date and the current TOD (time of day).

Selecting the Date & TOD

  • Tap the date at the top to change it
  • Tap the TOD selector to switch between parade states:

💡 Each TOD is saved separately. You can go back to any TOD to view or edit it (if not yet submitted).
✅ Marking Attendance

Tap the Platoon tab (Platoon 1 / Platoon 2 / HQ) to switch between platoons. Personnel are grouped by section.

Setting a Status

1
Quick buttons

Each row has quick-tap buttons for the most common statuses (Present, Duty, MC, etc.). Tap once to assign.

2
More statuses

Tap More… on a person's row to see all available statuses. Scroll down the list to find less common ones.

3
Date-range statuses (MC, Leave, etc.)

After selecting a date-range status, enter the From and Till dates. The status will auto-carry to other TODs and future days within the range.

Clearing Statuses

Tap ✕ Clear [Platoon] Statuses at the bottom of a platoon section, then confirm. This resets everyone in that platoon to unassigned.

⚠ Clearing is irreversible. Only clear if you need to start over for that platoon.

Set All Present

Tap the ✓ All Present button to mark everyone in the platoon as Present in one tap. Useful when almost everyone is in — then individually update those who are not.

🔄 Auto Carry-Forward

The app automatically fills in statuses when you switch to a different TOD, so you don't have to re-enter everything.

Whole-Day Duty (carries across TODs same day)

Once set at HLS/FP, these statuses automatically appear in MP, LP, and Night Strength:

DUTY DTO ADTL DOO DTL

Date-Range Statuses (carries across days)

Statuses with a from/till date carry across all TODs for all days within the range:

MC HL OL LL ARF AMB T1 AMB T2 and all other leave statuses

Night Strength (S/O)

Personnel with S/O or S/O Till (within date range) are automatically marked at the 2030/2230 TOD.

💡 Carry only fills in people who have no status yet for that slot. If you manually set a different status, it will not be overwritten.
⚠️ Expiry Alerts

A yellow banner appears at the top of the attendance screen when someone's leave or status is ending soon.

IconMeaning
🔴Status ends today
🟡Status ends tomorrow

For S/O Till entries the banner also shows the reporting time:

🔴 LCP JAKIN — Stay Out Till ends tdy, back tmr 0800 (S/O)
💡 Use this banner as a reminder to follow up — confirm the person is extending their MC/leave, or update their status for the next day.
✍️ Submitting Attendance

Once all statuses for a platoon are filled in, scroll to the bottom of that platoon tab and tap:

Save & Submit [Platoon] Attendance

  • The platoon is locked — no further changes can be made
  • A green Edit button appears if you need to unlock and make corrections (admin / authorised users only)
  • Submit each platoon separately (P1, P2, HQ)
⚠ Submit only when all statuses are confirmed. Submitting early will lock the platoon and require admin override to edit.
📤 Telegram Messages

Tap the Telegram tab (bottom nav) to generate messages. There are three message types:

📤 Node Message

Full parade state for the whole node. Sent to the node group chat. Shows total strength, duty count, and all statuses with names.

  • You can adjust the Total Node Strength manually if needed
  • Add Additional Notes that appear at the end of the message

👑 Comd Message

Commanders-only view (3SG and above). Shows only commanders' statuses. Sent to the commanders' group chat.

🏥 MC List

Lists all personnel currently on MC or Hospitalisation Leave. Format:

Node: JRN Date: 130426 MC 1. LCP JAKIN, PLT 2 TO BASE MC 130426 - 130426 2. PTE BRAXTON, PLT 1 TO BASE HL 110426 - 310526

Tap 📋 Copy Message for Telegram to copy, then paste into the Telegram group.

💡 The message is generated live from current attendance records. Make sure statuses are filled in before copying.
🌙 S/O Consolidated List

Tap the 🌙 button in the top header of the attendance screen to open the Stay Out list.

This shows all personnel currently on S/O or S/O Till, grouped by platoon, with:

  • Permanent S/O badge
  • Temporary S/O Till — end date and days remaining
💡 Use this as a quick reference before Night Strength to know who will be auto-marked S/O.
📋 Nominal Roll (NR) Mode

NR mode is for events like CST, IPPT, or any activity where you need a separate attendance record outside the normal parade state.

Starting an NR

1
Tap NR on the home screen

Select Nominal Roll from the TOD options.

2
Enter the event name

E.g. "IPPT", "CST", "SOC". This appears in the NR telegram message header.

3
Mark attendance normally

Mark each person as Present or with a reason. Submit each platoon when done.

NR Telegram Message

The NR tab generates a formatted CAA message:

IPPT CAA 13 Apr 26 Total No. Of Strength: 42 Total No. Of Commanders including TLs : 14 Total No. Of NSFs excluding TLs : 28 —————— Reason For Not Attending: MC (Medical Certificate): 2 1. LCP JAKIN 2. PTE BRAXTON —————— Commanders Completed: 13/14 (93%) NSFs Completed: 27/28 (96%) JRN Completion rate: 95%
💡 SCT and above (including 3SG, 2SG, etc.) are counted as Commanders. REC to CFC are counted as NSFs.
🕓 History

Tap History in the bottom nav to view past parade states.

  • Tap a date to Preview the parade state PDF
  • Tap Edit to go back to that date's attendance (admin only for past dates)
  • Download a date range as a bulk PDF (admin feature)
💡 History is read-only for normal users. Contact your admin if you need to correct a past record.
💡 Quick Tips
  • Always mark attendance at HLS/FP first — duty statuses carry forward automatically
  • For MC/Leave, always enter the From date (first day sick) and Till date (last day, i.e. the MC date) so carry-forward works correctly
  • Check the expiry banner at the start of each parade state — it flags anyone whose MC or leave ends that day
  • Do not submit until you are sure — submitted platoons are locked
  • If a status looks wrong after switching TODs, check if carry-forward applied — you can override by tapping the person and changing their status
